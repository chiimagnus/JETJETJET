# JETJETJET Privacy Policy

**Effective Date: 2025-08-08**

## 1. Introduction

Welcome to JETJETJET (hereinafter referred to as "the App" or "we"). We understand how important your personal information is to you, and we are committed to protecting your privacy and personal information security. This Privacy Policy is designed to explain how we collect, use, store, and protect your information, as well as your rights.

Please read this policy carefully before using the App. If you do not agree with any content of this policy, please stop using the App immediately.

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Sensor Data
Only when you actively start recording, the App will collect motion data generated by CoreMotion locally (such as acceleration, angular velocity, magnetic field, etc.) for 3D real-time display and playback functions.

### 2.2 Flight Records and Metadata
Flight sessions and related metadata (such as duration, title, number of data points, etc.) that you actively save within the app.

### 2.3 Local Errors and Logs
To troubleshoot and fix local issues, the app may save error messages or running logs locally on the device, but will not upload them to any server.

### 2.4 Important Statement
- The App **will not** actively collect device model, operating system version, or app version for continuous data collection
- If temporary troubleshooting is needed, we may record some operational information as needed and only save it locally on the device for debugging purposes, and will not upload or share it in any form
- The App **will not** upload any user sensor data, flight records, device information, or local logs to developer-controlled servers or third-party services in any form
- The App **will not** automatically collect or send any user data in the background

## 3. How Information is Collected

### 3.1 Real-time Collection
The app will only collect sensor data when you explicitly start recording or allow related functions.

### 3.2 Local Storage
By default, all recorded and generated data is saved locally on the device.

### 3.3 Cloud Sync
The App does not provide iCloud/CloudKit cloud sync functionality, and data is not transmitted through the cloud.

## 4. Purpose of Information Use

We only use the collected information for the following purposes:
- Providing core functions: Implementing real-time 3D display, recording playback, and history management
- Local troubleshooting and repair

## 5. Information Sharing and Disclosure

### 5.1 Third-party Services
The App **does not use** any third-party analytics or crash reporting services, and will not sell or rent personal identification information to third parties.

### 5.2 Cloud Sync Services
The App does not provide iCloud/CloudKit synchronization, and data is not transmitted through third-party services.

### 5.3 Legal Requirements
We may disclose your information when required by applicable laws or to protect our rights, property, or safety, or that of others.

## 6. Data Storage and Deletion

### 6.1 Local Data
Recordings and historical data are saved locally on the device, and you can manually delete any records within the app.

### 6.2 Cloud Sync Data
Not applicable (the App does not provide iCloud/CloudKit sync).

### 6.3 Retention Period
We will retain your records during the period you keep the data; if you delete records, we will clear them.

## 7. Your Rights

### 7.1 Access and Deletion
You can access and delete your flight records at any time within the app.

### 7.2 Data Portability
You can export your data in a structured, commonly used and machine-readable format.

### 7.3 Withdraw Consent
Not applicable (the App does not provide cloud sync functionality).

## 8. Security Measures

We implement reasonable technical and management measures to protect data security (such as managing read/write permissions according to Apple platform recommendations). However, please note that no network or storage system can guarantee 100% security, and we will strive to protect your information security.

## 9. Children's Protection

This App is not designed for children under 13 years of age. If parents or guardians believe that a child has provided personal information to us, please contact us to request deletion.

## 10. Privacy Policy Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Major changes will be published in the app or repository and the effective date will be updated. Please check this page regularly for the latest information.

## 11. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or need assistance, please contact us through the following methods:

### 2.1 传感器数据
仅在您主动开始录制时，本应用会在本地采集 CoreMotion 产生的运动数据（如加速度、角速度、磁场等），用于实现 3D 实时显示与回放功能。

### 2.2 飞行记录与元数据
您在应用内主动保存的飞行会话及相关元信息（如时长、标题、数据点数等）。

### 2.3 本地错误与日志
为排查和修复本地问题，应用可能在设备本地保存错误消息或运行日志，但不会上传至任何服务器。

### 2.4 重要声明
- 本应用**不会**主动收集设备型号、操作系统版本或应用版本用于持续数据收集
- 如需临时排查问题，可能按需记录部分运行信息并仅保存在设备本地用于调试，且不会以任何形式上传或共享
- 本应用**不会**以任何形式将用户的传感器数据、飞行记录、设备信息或本地日志上传到开发者控制的服务器或第三方服务
